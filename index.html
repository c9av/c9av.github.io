Our Story

C9 Audio Visual started officially in 2017 as the culmination of one young man's lifelong obsession with audiovisual entertainment. Hi! I am Matthew Carnein, founder and owner of C9 Audio Visual. Today, C9AV has grown to include a vast community of talented freelance technicians, partner companies, nonprofit organizations, and talented artists. C9AV owns and maintains an ever growing inventory of state-of-the-art AV equipment, while also having insider access to equipment of several of our partner companies throughout all of New England. Please let us know how we can help take your event or installation to the next level!
